Frequently Asked Questions

How is the Digital Parenting Score calculated? ▼
The score is based on research-backed guidelines for children's digital wellness. It considers sleep duration, outdoor play, study balance, and screen time management. Each factor contributes to a score out of 100.
Is my child's data stored anywhere? ▼
No. All calculations happen entirely in your browser. We never collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you or your child.
What age group is this calculator for? ▼
This calculator is designed for children aged 3-17 years. Recommendations are adjusted based on age-appropriate guidelines.
Is this a medical assessment? ▼
No, this is an educational tool only. It provides general guidance based on common digital wellness principles and should not replace professional medical or psychological advice.

About This Tool

We help Indian parents understand their child's digital habits and make informed decisions about screen time management. In today's digital age, parents face unprecedented challenges in managing their children's screen time and digital consumption.

Our tool provides quick, actionable insights based on research-backed guidelines for healthy digital habits. It evaluates multiple aspects of a child's daily routine including sleep, outdoor activity, study balance, and screen time across different platforms.
